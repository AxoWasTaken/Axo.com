AXR Law

This document covers the legal system of the Axos Republic and any other entities under the same administration.

Civil Offences

These laws cover smaller non-criminal offences, such as trespassing or stealing lower-level goods.

Trespassing

  • 1.1.1 - Anyone may enter another’s property, but they must leave if asked by the property owner.
  • 1.1.2 - Refusing to leave another’s property is prohibited, and may result in punishment.
  • 1.1.3 - Killing, or harming a trespasser over 10 damage points is prohibited, and may result in greater punishment than the trespasser.
  • 1.1.4 - Entering a restricted union zone without permission from at least a commander is prohibited and will result in punishment.

Freedom of Speech

  • 1.2.1 - Insulting the Lord is only prohibited if made in serious sense, not if made as part of a joke or sketch.
  • 1.2.2 - Insulting the Admiral is always prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.2.3 - Insulting a general is always prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.2.4 - Insulting a commander is only prohibited if made in a serious sense, not if made as part of a joke or sketch.
  • 1.2.5 - Insulting a soldier is only prohibited if made in a serious sense, not if made as part of a joke or sketch.
  • 1.2.6 - Insulting a civilian or equal no matter rank is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.2.7 - Insulting a person of lower rank is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 1.2.8 - Using the words: Skibidi, Alpha (As class), Beta (As class), Sigma (As class), and Omega (As class). Any other definitively brain rot words are prohibited.
  • 1.2.9 - Racism and sexism are strictly prohibited and will most definitely result in punishment.
  • 1.2.10 - Leaking classified information or media is strictly prohibited and will result in severe punishment. This includes reading, buying, owning, or giving the media.

Building

  • 1.3.1 – Building inappropriate structures that resemble a Swastika, or a male reproductive organ is strictly prohibited and can result in great punishment. The soviet hammer and sickle is an exception.
  • 1.3.2 – Flying the flag of an official enemy of the state is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.3.3 – Buildings must not exceed 10 blocks without a permit; such can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 1.3.4 – Extending or branching a road adjacent to your property is not prohibited but it cannot be classed or named without a permit, such can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 1.3.5 – Constructing a cellar or basement is not prohibited unless there is already a man-made chamber or Redstone cable.
  • 1.3.6 – Mining under your property is prohibited unless you have a permit, such can be obtained from the Lord. Instead of making your own mine use a publicly accessible one.
  • 1.3.7 – Extending or branching an adjacent railway, canal, or ice track is prohibited and may result in punishment. This does not apply if you build an isolated one on your property.
  • 1.3.8 – Littering on the public domain is prohibited and may result in punishment depending on severity and history.
  • 1.3.9 – Littering on another’s property is prohibited and may result in punishment.

Transport

  • 1.4.1 – When crossing railways check there are no carts coming to ensure the safety of you and the passengers/cargo. You hold full liability for any accidents you are responsible for.
  • 1.4.2 – Blocking the ice tracks is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.4.3 – Damaging the ice tracks is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.4.4 – Trying to sail boats on R class roads is prohibited but cannot be punished.

Redstone

  • 1.5.1 – Building flying machines on your property is prohibited without a permit and may result in punishment. Such can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 1.5.2 – Using any form of wireless transmission in your projects is prohibited without a permit, such can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 1.5.3 – Any projects that use noteblocks or jukeboxes are prohibited without a permit, such can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 1.5.4 – Any projects that contain explosives are strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 1.5.5 – Lag machines are strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.

Criminal Offences

These laws cover larger offences such as murder or terrorism.

Murder

  • 2.1.1 – Killing another player is strictly prohibited without general rank and good reason. This does not count for PvP related competitions however they are also prohibited without a permit.
  • 2.1.2 – Inspiring, ordering or bribing another player to kill is strictly prohibited and will result in equal punishment to the actual murder.
  • 2.1.3 – Indirectly killing using a timed bomb, pitfall, etc. Is strictly prohibited and will result in severe punishment.
  • 2.1.4 – Killing villagers is prohibited and may result in great punishment.

Terrorism

  • 2.2.1 – Placing a TNT cart on the railway is strictly prohibited and doing so classes you as a terrorist. It is impossible to do such accidentally and will always be punished.
  • 2.2.2 – Intentionally jamming telegraph lines is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.2.3 – Sending off flying machines to crash into any structure is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.2.4 – Planting landmines without a permit is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment. Such permit can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 2.2.5 – Burning down any building without a permit is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment. Such permit can be obtained from the Lord.
  • 2.2.6 – Kidnapping any player is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.2.7 – Making, buying, selling, or owning any form of explosive substance is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.

Theft

  • 2.3.1 – Stealing an item from another player without their permission is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.3.2 – Stealing money from another player is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.3.3 – Stealing the Lord’s crown is strictly prohibited and may result in severe punishment.

Fraud

  • 2.4.1 – Stealing the identity of another player is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.4.2 – Forging money is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.4.3 – Forging permits is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.4.4 – Forging AXR documents is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.4.5 – Forging a fossil or large ore vein is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.4.6 – Pretending to have fought in the Rubicona War is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.4.7 – Forging ancient structures or shipwrecks is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.

Protection

  • 2.5.1 – Cutting down trees in a protected area is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.5.2 – Killing a protected creature or plant is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.5.3 – Removing lava from a protected area is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.5.4 – Damaging a protected site is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.5.5 – Mining in a protected area is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.5.6 – Building in a protected area is strictly prohibited and may result in punishment.

Treason

  • 2.6.1 – Protest and counter-protest are not prohibited unless they turn violent.
  • 2.6.2 – Disobeying orders from commanders is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • 2.6.3 – Disobeying orders from generals is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.6.4 – Disobeying orders from the Allegiant General, Admiral, or Lord is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.6.5 – Starting a riot is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • 2.6.6 – Becoming a rogue against the AXR is strictly prohibited and may result in severe punishment.
  • 2.6.7 – Attempting or succeeding a split of the AXR is strictly prohibited and may result in severe punishment. This applies to all sides of the split.
